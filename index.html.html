09 Growth Drivers & Challenges Source: IMARC Group · MoSPI · IBEF · IDC India 2025

Key Growth Drivers

  • Rising household income — avg ₹3.19 lakh/yr (McKinsey 2025); per capita NNI ₹1,92,774 (MoSPI FY25)
  • Affluent population doubling to 8.8 crore by 2028 (UBS / IBEF) — direct luxury demand catalyst
  • E-commerce — 63.4% of wearable shipments via online platforms; growing Tier 2/3 reach
  • Premiumisation in analog — Titan analog +26% YoY Q2 FY25; Ethos ASP up 7% to ₹2.07 lakh
  • Deep gifting culture — watches remain among top gifting categories (corporate and personal)
  • Growing women workforce (41%) driving fastest-growing end-user segment at 9.16% CAGR
  • Fashion brand collaborations — Titan-Ducati, Helios-U-BOAT, international brand entry
  • Swiss watch exports to India rose 12.7% in H1 2025 — luxury appetite confirmed
  • Pre-owned / certified luxury market emerging — Tata CLiQ ReLoved, Jay's Watch Store

Key Challenges

  • Unorganised market undercuts organised players by 60–80% in Tier 3+ cities and rural markets
  • Counterfeit goods — grey market valued at ₹1.4 lakh crore nationally, significant watch component
  • 30% import duty on luxury watches raises India prices above global market levels
  • Smartphone cannibalising casual watch demand among Gen Z consumers
  • Smartwatch shipments fell 34.4% in 2024 (IDC) — signalling wearable market consolidation
  • Heavy import dependence on Swiss movements and components creates cost pressure
  • Limited differentiation in budget segment suppressing volume growth at mass-market end
10 Recent Developments — 2024–2025
Sep 2025 Samsung launched Galaxy Watch 8 and Watch 8 Classic in India with enhanced health and wellness features — pricing starting at ₹32,999. Marks continued premiumisation in the digital segment.
Aug 2025 Casio India unveiled its first locally produced automatic watch collection — Edifice EFK-100 series — priced from ₹25,995. Signals Casio's move into the mechanical segment in India.
Dec 2025 Titan Company partnered with Ducati India — 43 watch models priced between ₹14,799 and ₹27,999. Brand collaboration strategy accelerating Titan's premium positioning.
Sep 2024 Titan Helios partnered with Italian premium watchmaker U-BOAT to unveil watches in India. Helios network: 240 stores across 95 cities and growing.
Apr 2025 Xiaomi launched Redmi Watch Move — India's first locally manufactured smartwatch at ₹1,999. Reflects PLI-driven domestic manufacturing momentum in the budget wearable segment.
Jan 2024 Tata CLiQ Luxury launched the ReLoved pre-owned certified watch store in partnership with Jay's Watch Store — offering Rolex, Omega, and other premium brands. Pre-owned segment formalising.
H1 2025 Swiss watch exports to India reached CHF 128.3 million — up 12.7% YoY and +30.2% vs H1 2023 (Federation of the Swiss Watch Industry). Rolex, Omega, Breitling expanding mono-brand boutiques.
